02. Master Batch

Color masterbatch (colored plastic granules, also known as color concentrate granules), are raw materials used to color finished plastic products, thereby satisfying the aesthetic requirements. The process of producing colored plastic granules starts from mixing high-quality pigments, base resins and some special additives, through the granulation processing process, the final product is color masterbatch particles.

View more

03. Fillers

Our Globalene transparent Filler masterbatch is a high-performance additive specifically designed to enhance the mechanical properties and cost-effectiveness of various plastic products while maintaining excellent transparency. Formulated with premium sodium sulfate as the primary filler and a compatible carrier resin, this masterbatch is ideal for applications requiring clarity and durability, such as packaging films, sheets, and injection-molded products.
