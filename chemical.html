Chemicals

    • Chemicals

      • Our chemical division extends its reach around the globe in support of our customers’ needs.With our product knowledge and product availability we minimize your effort when choosing prime or reclaimed materials for your production process
      1. Plasticizer
      • Plasticizer is a material added to plastics, including PVC, to enhance flexibility and other various physical properties, and are used in wallpapers, floor materials, electric cables, artificial leathers, etc.
        • DOP
        • General purpose (for various purposes): Flexible sheet, tarpaulin, urethane additives, stabilizer, etc.
        • TOTM
        • Electric cable compounds (that require especially high heat resistance), high pressure hoses, medical blood bags, etc.
        • DINP
        • General purpose (for various purposes): Flexible sheet, electric cable compounds, automotive sealers, tarpaulins, hoses, etc.
        • NEO-T (DOTP)
        • General purpose (used for various purposes): Sheet, flooring materials, artificial leather, gasket, etc.
      2. Polyurethane (PU)
      • Polyurethane PU is made by mixing a Polyol with a different diisocyanate, e.g Toluene diisocyanate (TDI) or Methylenediphenyl diisocyanate (MDI ) with other chemical and additives to produce materials that can be used in furniture/bedding, construction, insulation, automotive and CASE (coatings, adhesives, sealants and elastomers).
        • Isocyanate- diphenylmethane diisocyanate (MDI) and toluene diisocyanate (TDI)
        • Polyol
      3. Caustic Soda
      • Caustic soda flake, chemically known as sodium hydroxide (NaOH), is a versatile compound widely used in industrial processes. It appears as white flakes, easily soluble in water, and releases heat during dissolution.
      • Caustic soda flake is indispensable across diverse sectors:
      • Paper and Pulp: Used for pulping and bleaching.
      • Textiles: Essential for dyeing and fabric treatment.
      • Soap Manufacturing: A primary ingredient in soap and detergent production.
      • Water Treatment: Neutralizes acidity and purifies water.
      • Food Processing: Aids in peeling and chemical processing of certain foods.
